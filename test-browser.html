Manual Test - Check Browser Console

Open Browser DevTools (F12) and go to Console tab to see all console.log statements.

Then navigate in the iframe below:

  1. Click "Click to go OFFLINE (testing)" button at bottom-right
  2. Click on the "Offline Test List" in sidebar
  3. Click "Add Item" button
  4. Fill in "Offline Item 1" in Name field
  5. Click "Add" or press Enter
  6. Check console for [CreateItemDialog], [useCreateItem], [IndexedDB], etc. logs
